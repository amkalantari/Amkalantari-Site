Privacy Policy

Overview

Unitly (“the App”) is a unit conversion application developed by Amir Kalantari. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Unitly does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Payment information

The App may store limited data locally on your device, including:

  • Recent conversions
  • App preferences
  • Theme settings
  • Language preferences

This information remains on your device and is not used to identify you personally.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Approximate location
  • App interaction data

This information is used to provide and improve advertising services. For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

Analytics

The App may use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. Analytics information may include:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • General usage events

This information is collected in an aggregated and anonymous manner.

Crash Reporting

The App may use Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix application issues. Crash reports may include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Technical crash logs

No personal information is intentionally collected through crash reports.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Certain technical information may be processed by trusted third-party service providers, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase

These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect application data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact amkalantari.dev@gmail.com.
